Strategic Pivot: Construction vs. Mandate GTM

Traditional Stage-Gate frameworks focus on developer-level construction milestones (drawings, structural completion, slab casting). Under Anarock's Exclusive Mandate Model, our primary financial and operational risks lie in marketing delays, mispriced product structures, slow sales velocity, and rising Lead CAC.

This playbook pivots the Cooper Stage-Gate model to govern the sales launch and GTM execution lifecycle, integrating Anarock's proprietary AI suite to compress velocity targets.

Risk-Adjusted Stage-Gate Mitigations

Operational protocols designed to address launch delays and conversion anomalies before margin erosion occurs.

Risk Category Critical Stage Impact Severity Stage-Gate Mitigation Mechanism
RERA Certification Delays Stage 2 (Creative Prep) High Gate 2 requires a validated RERA certificate. Zero marketing spend is unlocked until the Karnataka RERA registration is active, shielding the developer from regulatory penalty exposure.
Broker Apathy / Low CP Pull Stage 2 (GTM Strategy) Medium If Gate 2 CP pre-registration falls below 150, CP Ranker triggers an auto-tiering incentive, raising payouts for top 5% brokers while utilizing CP Genie campaigns to re-mobilize the database.
Weak Soft-Booking EOI Corpus Stage 3 (Pre-Launch) High Gate 3 mandates a ₹12 Cr token pool. If unsatisfied, the public launch is postponed. Lead profiling scoring targets are recalibrated in Astra Platinum, and concierge MVP previews are expanded to corporate partners.
Acquisition CAC Inflation Stage 4 (Sustenance) Medium If weekly CAC climbs above 2.5% GTV, the system shifts digital spend to cold lead revival via Astra Phoenix. It triggers webhook workflows in n8n to re-engage cold leads via custom LLM prompts, bypassing fresh digital ad spends.
Overhang Absorption Lag Stage 4 (Sustenance) Low Weekly Sales Velocity Index (SVI) falling below 12 units triggers the activation of "Barbell pricing" adjustment. Sub-units are structured under flexible payment plans (e.g. 10-90 structure) to reset demand.
